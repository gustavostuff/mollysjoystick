DIY USB Arcade joystick

Tired of those bulky Arcade boxes occupying a lot of space? Want a high-quality, small enough joystick that fits perfectly on your desktop so you can play some games instead of working? Then Molly's Joystick is for you!

Presenting Molly's Joystick first generation

Rainbow, Iceberg and Dragon are the 3 current flavors. The acrylic part serving as wrist rest is completely optional, the sets can be assembled with or without it. Check out the following renders for the Micro size.

Not small enough you say? Try the Nano size! We don't have renders for it yet but you can kind of see the relative size down below.

3mm, laser cut acrylic parts

We also sell the case alone so, if you're thinking in something more custom-made like different colors, sanwa-like parts or anything, then this may be useful! The pack includes:

  • Acrylic parts
  • Screws
  • Nuts
  • Washers
